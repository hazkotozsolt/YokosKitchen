The history of Yoko's Kitchen

Yoko, born in a Japanese family, is an enthusiastic food lover, who likes to teach, just as much as she loves to cook. Everything started at her teenage years, when she first tried to cook something for her family, which turned out great. After that, she quickly developed it as a habit, and she wanted to turn her hobby, her passion into her dream job.


Yoko tried to dream big, and she founded her school, Yoko's Kitchen, with the support of her family and friends in 2011.


Since then, she managed to teach over 1000+ happy hobby chefs about the japanese cousine, some of them even managed to change careers.
